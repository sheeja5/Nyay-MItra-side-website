About Nyay Mitra

In India, legal help is out of reach for many. With only 1.3 lawyers per 1,000 people and high costs, people in rural areas and small cities face big challenges—like family disputes or shop complaints—without guidance.

Our Features

Find a Lawyer

Connect with affordable, nearby lawyers for issues like property disputes. Tell us your problem and budget, and we’ll match you!

NyaySathi (AI Helper)

Ask our 24/7 chatbot questions like “How do I file a complaint?” in English, Hindi, or other languages. It’s like a legal expert in your pocket!

Document Maker

Create legal papers (e.g., affidavits, complaints) for just ₹50–100 by filling simple forms—way cheaper than ₹2,000!

Tips & News

Get easy tips like “Your rights if cheated” and updates on new laws to stay informed.
